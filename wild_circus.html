About Us

The Wild Circus show is a wild and amazing event. Our show is funny, crazy and awesome.
Created back in 1978 in Pérouges, a small village near Lyon in France, the company was founded by 3 friends: a clown, a magician and a feline tamer.
After a few years, our company had grown up and was giving performances all around the world but we always stuck to our original specialties: making you laugh out loud with clowns, striking you speechless with magicians, and amazing you with the felines.

Come and meet us for a wild moment. Take off your shoes to be comfortable and enjoy!
